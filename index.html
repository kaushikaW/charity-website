Who We Are

Sri Lanka has many disadvantaged and forgotten children, particularly in the countryside and orphans
The Giving Hand charity foundation has been created to gather money, resources and volunteers to improve the life chances of these children.

Our mission

Our foundation's Mission is to improve life chances for underprivileged children in Sri Lanka

Our vision

A world in which every child attains the right to survival, protection, development and participation.

Our Partners

We can Say our foundation is successful and Trustworthy. Most international and national organisations Like UNICEF, UNS, National child care Organization help and support us.

Our Achievements

Educational Programs

We have arranged more than 50 educational programs to teach English, Math, and I.T to children who live in rural areas without good education background. Our foundation has more than 50 volunteer teachers.

Educational Resources

We have received more than Rs.500 000 worth of educational goods from our kind hatred donators. And we would like to say proudly more than 5000 children use those goods for their learnings.

Aiding Development

Right now we have completed more than 10 projects on building I.T labs , Music Rooms, and drinking water supplies for rural area schools

Nutrition Programs

We are conducting more than 20 nutrition projects in 20 rural schools aiming for junior students to fulfil their nutritional needs.

How Can You help Us

Donate funds as you can. We will manage them and work to share with poor children's education and food needs.
Donate Educational materials (Books, pencils. etc), clothes, and valuables stuff for children
You can register as a volunteer teacher. Share your knowledge with the children who are unable to have a proper education.
